Texas Electricity Rates 2026: Compare Plans & Save Up to 40%

Published March 2, 2026 · 13.Energy · 8 min read

Why Texas Energy Rates Matter

Texas has a deregulated electricity market, meaning you can choose your provider. About 85% of Texans have the power to choose — and those who compare plans save 20-40% on average. But with 100+ providers and 500+ plans, choosing can be overwhelming. This guide cuts through the noise.

How to Compare Texas Electricity Plans

  1. Check your current rate on your latest electricity bill. Look for the $/kWh line
  2. Know your usage — average Texas home uses 1,100-1,200 kWh/month
  3. Read the EFL (Electricity Facts Label) — this is required by Texas law and shows the true cost at 500, 1000, and 2000 kWh usage levels
  4. Watch for hidden fees — base charges, minimum usage fees, and early termination penalties
  5. Compare at YOUR usage level — some plans are cheap at 1000kWh but expensive at 500kWh due to minimum usage charges

  • Reliant Energy: NRG subsidiary, strong customer service, good rewards programs
  • Green Mountain Energy: 100% renewable plans, competitive pricing, carbon neutral
  • 4Change Energy: Budget-friendly, donates 4% of revenue to charity
  • Chariot Energy: 100% solar plans, no gimmicks, transparent pricing
  • Pulse Power: Low fixed rates, simple plans, growing fast

When to Switch Providers

The best time to switch is 2-4 weeks before your contract expires. Texas law allows you to switch providers at any time, but early termination fees ($50-$200) may apply if you break a contract early.

Market timing: Rates are typically lowest in March-May and September-November (shoulder seasons). Avoid signing contracts during summer peak demand (June-August) when rates are 15-30% higher.
